An intensive LA-style Salsa afternoon with workshop and practice time at **Inflow Studio** — a central location with great air conditioning and a wooden dance floor.

## Program

- **15:00 – 15:45** — Workshop: LA-Style technique
- **15:45 – 16:15** — Free practice time
- **16:15 – 17:00** — Workshop: Intermediate

**LA-Style technique** is an open-level workshop focused on technique. Everyone will have something to work on, from improver to upper intermediate level.

**Intermediate workshop** — to benefit, you should be comfortable with salsa patterns (roughly 6 months of dancing or more), or have taken our improver-level class before. If in doubt, reach out!

## Prices

- One workshop and practice time: **100 DKK**
- Two workshops and practice time: **150 DKK**

## Good to know

Maximum 20 participants. Please arrive a few minutes early — classes start on time. Car parking is not allowed in the courtyard; public paid parking is available on Heimdalsgade. Outdoor shoes or shoes with heels (that can scratch or leave marks on the floor) are not allowed inside the studios.
