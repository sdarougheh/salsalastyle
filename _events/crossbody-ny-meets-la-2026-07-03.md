Crossbody Salsa refers to all the linear variants of salsa — LA-Style (on1) and NY-Style (on2) — which aren't danced as much in Copenhagen. We provide an environment for crossbody dancers to meet each other and dance all night long. Whether you dance on1 or on2, this is your night. **All levels welcome!**

## Program

- **20:00 – 20:30** — LA-Style Workshop (open level). Need some crossbody inspiration? Pick up some new patterns for the night.
- **20:30 – 23:00** — Social dancing. 90% crossbody salsa, 10% cha-cha-cha.

Held in Studio A of Inflow Studio — centrally located, with a wonderful wooden dance floor.

## Entry

**70 DKK**, paid via MobilePay at the door or to box **2244AK** ([pay here](https://qr.mobilepay.dk/box/0e87a242-413a-4d41-8b99-0025af193ddc/pay-in)).

## Good to know

To access the studio, enter through the gate to the courtyard; the building door is then to your right. Car parking is not allowed in the courtyard; public paid parking is available on Heimdalsgade. Outdoor shoes or shoes that can scratch or leave marks on the floor are not allowed inside the studios.
