Interested in learning or refreshing your LA-Style Salsa? If you have some experience in Cuban salsa (or any other couple dance), then this workshop is for you! It's also for you if you want to **switch roles** — you know how to lead, but have never followed, or the other way around — or if you haven't danced LA-style salsa for a while and need a refresher.

In this two-hour workshop (with a break), we take you through all of the main LA patterns, with a special focus on technique and how it differs from Cuban Salsa. We cover the emphasis, feeling, and leading, and go through the main steps: the **cross-body lead**, **left and right turns**, and the **titanic** — with time at the end for practice.

This workshop also sets you up for the improver or intermediate-level classes starting **September 2nd**: use it to figure out whether you'd prefer to do the basics in more detail, or skip to the higher level.
