Improvers This class is perfect for Salsa dancers who can comfortable dance left and right turns in Cuban salsa, or who have good experience in other partner dances. We will cover the difference between Cuban and LA-Style, practice left and right turns (LA-style), and cross-body leads. We will also do many fun variations (such as arm tosses) that are relatively easy to implement, but useful also for more experienced dancers.

Guided Practica The guided practica is accessible only for our students, at no extra cost. We will play slow to medium tempo songs with clear rhythm. Come and practice what you learned in class -- the teachers will be around to give you feedback!

Intermediate 1 The class covers covering inside and outside turns, 360, and other intemerdiate concepts. We focus on connection and technique, making sure that followers don't predict the patterns during the class, so that we can easily apply what we learned to social dances.

Intermediate 2 This class builds on the material from Intermediate 1 and introduces more difficult techniques such as double turns. We learn additional combinations and put even more effort on technique and body positioning so that the combinations work effortless with faster music.

If you have not been with us before, contact us before signing up for Intermediate 2.

Requirements

LA-style basics match Cuban Salsa. We recommend beginner classes at 4water or CSA first.

  • Open level Minimum requirement: Improver. Technique-focused workshops benefiting all levels.
  • Improver 3-6 months Cuban Salsa; comfortable with beat, lead/follow left/right turns.
  • Intermediate Can lead/follow LA-style turns, cross-body leads, open breaks, titanic.
  • Advanced Can lead/follow inside turns, outside turns, and single fast spins.
