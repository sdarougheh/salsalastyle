LA-style Salsa (also called on1) is a Salsa style that originated in the North American ballrooms. Unlike Cuban Salsa, the couple dances in a linear pattern rather than circular. It's very popular across Europe, except Copenhagen—we're here to change that!

Requirements

LA-style basics match Cuban Salsa. We recommend beginner classes at 4water or CSA first.

  • Open level: Minimum Improver. Technique-focused workshops benefiting all levels.
  • Improver: 3-6 months Cuban Salsa; comfortable with beat, lead/follow left/right turns.
  • Intermediate 1: Can lead/follow LA-style turns, cross-body leads, open breaks, titanic.
  • Intermediate 2: Builds on Intermediate 1. Strongly recommend taking both together.
